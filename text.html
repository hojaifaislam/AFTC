SilverTape™ Acrylic Foam Tapes

AFTC SilverTapes are double sided viscoelastic 100% acrylic foam tapes (AFT) . Because AFTC SilverTapes are 100% solid acrylic adhesive, they are extremely durable providing outstanding age and weather resistance.

Registered trademark

SilverTape™ is AFTC® Group’s registered trademark and is AFTC® Group’s core product. SilverTapes are double sided viscoelastic 100% acrylic Foam tapes (AFT) . Because SilverTapes are 100% solid acrylic adhesive, they are extremely durable providing outstanding age and weather resistance. This makes SilverTape™ the perfect match for the bonding of many different materials such as aluminium, steel, plastics and glass. But also low surface energy materials like ASA, ABS, PP/PE EPDM, POM and many more.

75 different types

The viscoelastic property of SilverTape™ ensures a constant fluent state. This fluency means the tape flows into all pores of the material and ‘locks’ itself in the material for an unmatched bond. Besides that, in addition when under pressure, the tape sees its strength from its elasticity, and the viscosity also ensures a low internal tension. Because of these properties, SilverTape™, in contrast to foam (PE, PU, polyester etc.) backed tape has the ability to withstand much higher static and dynamic forces.

Heat Activated SilverTape™:

Due to cost reduction, the automotive industry started exploring material savings in every direction. Weather seals used to be pressed onto an aluminium rail on the bodywork and doors of the car. Nowadays, these rails have disappeared and have been replaced by heat activated tapes. The weather seals are now directly bonded to the doors and bodywork. AFTC® has closely followed this development and has simultaneously developed, based on different paint and clear coats, four different grades of heat activated tapes.
